Lionel Messi Tribute

Lionel Andrés Messi, born 24 June 1987, also known as Leo Messi, is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for and captains both Major League Soccer club Inter Miami and the Argentina national team. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time, Messi has won a record seven Ballon d'Or awards and a record six European Golden Shoes, and in 2020 he was named to the Ballon d'Or Dream Team. Until leaving the club in 2021, he had spent his entire professional career with Barcelona, where he won a club-record 34 trophies, including ten La Liga titles, seven Copa del Rey titles and the UEFA Champions League four times. With his country, he won the 2021 Copa América and the 2022 FIFA World Cup.

Notable Achievements

  • 7 time FIFA Ballon d'Or winner
  • 34 total titles with FC Barcelona
  • 4 UEFA Champions League titles
  • 10 La Liga titles
  • 7 Copa del Rey titles
  • 2022 FIFA World Cup Champion
  • 2021 Copa America Champion
  • 800+ Record goals for FC Barcelona and Argentina
